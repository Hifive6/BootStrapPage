Video Game Information

This is where you would find very little gaming info on these games, Please look around

Legend of Zelda, Majora's Mask

Majora's Mask is set in Termina, an alternate reality to Ocarina of Time's Hyrule, which is to be destroyed along with its inhabitants in three days by a giant falling moon unless the player, as Link, can save it from imminent doom.

Borderlands 3

The plot is stopping the cult-leading twins, Troy and Tyreen Calypso, from harnessing the power of the alien Vaults. Players complete quests and side missions. When killed, enemies may drop weapons and gear which can be equipped.

Red Dead Redemption 2

The story is set in 1899 in a fictionalized representation of the Western, Midwestern and Southern United States and followes Arthur Morgan. Arthur deals with the decline of the Wild West and tries to survive the government forces, rival gangs, and other adversaries.

Halo: Combat Evolved

Halo is a game where players assume the role of the Master Chief, a cybernetically enhanced supersoldier, who is accompanied by Cortana (AI). Master Chief battle aliens as they uncover the secrets of the Halo, a ring-shaped artificial world.

Super Mario Bros 3

Players control plumbers Mario or Luigi, who must save Princess Toadstool and the rulers of seven different kingdoms from the antagonist Bowser. The Mushroom World, the setting of the game, is invaded by the Koopalings, Bowser's seven children.

Grand Theft Auto III

Set in fictional Liberty City, the game follows a criminal named Claude after he is left for dead and becomes entangled in a world of gangs, crime and corruption. The game is played from a third-person perspective.The open world design lets players freely roam.
